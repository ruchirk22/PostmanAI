How to Find Your Workspace ID

1. In Postman, go to the "Workspaces" dropdown in the top-left.

2. Select the workspace containing your collections.

3. In the main panel, click the information icon (i) next to the workspace name.

4. The Workspace ID will be listed in the panel that opens on the right. You can copy it from there.

Note: This is optional. If you leave it blank, the tool will fetch collections from all your workspaces.
